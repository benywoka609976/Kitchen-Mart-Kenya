About Kitchen Mart Kenya

Meet Our Founder

Welcome to Kitchen Mart Kenya! I'm Achila Benard, the founder and CEO of this venture. With over [X] years of experience in the kitchenware industry, I recognized the need for a reliable, customer-focused kitchen products supplier in Kenya.

My vision was to create a platform where Kenyans could access high-quality kitchen appliances, utensils, and accessories at affordable prices, with the added security of cash on delivery. We understand the concerns many have about online payments, which is why we've built our business around the cash on delivery model.

At Kitchen Mart Kenya, we're not just selling products - we're helping families create memorable meals and experiences in their kitchens. Thank you for trusting us with your kitchen needs.

To provide Kenyan households with access to high-quality, affordable kitchen products through a secure and convenient shopping experience. We're committed to enhancing kitchen experiences across Kenya by offering reliable products backed by exceptional customer service and our unique cash on delivery system.
